Quick Help

Navigation
Use the left sidebar (or the bottom tabs on mobile) to move between Dashboard, Candidates, Clients, Pipeline, Reports, and Settings.

Records
Click any row or card to open the full record. Use the Details / Activity / Chatter / Related tabs at the top of a record to navigate without scrolling.

Search
Use the search bar in the top bar to jump straight to a candidate or client by name or email.

Sync
Click "⟳ Sync" anytime to pull new applicants in from wepursu.one.

Insights
Check the Insights tab on your Dashboard for candidates and clients that may need attention — hot leads gone quiet, stalled applicants, and clients due for a check-in.
