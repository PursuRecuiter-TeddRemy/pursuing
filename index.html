The Story

Changing the narrative

After years working inside the staffing and workforce industry, Tedd C. Remy saw the same problems play out over and over again. Candidates were being shuffled through systems that never took the time to understand who they actually were. Clients were handed warm bodies instead of strategic workforce solutions. And somewhere in the middle, the human element — the part that actually makes a placement work — kept getting lost.

Tedd founded Pursu Solutions with a clear intention: to do it differently. Not just as a staffing agency, but as a true workforce partner — one willing to have the harder conversations, ask the deeper questions, and invest in outcomes rather than just transactions.

“There are people in this world who just need one person to believe in their potential before the rest of the world catches on. I built Pursu to be that person.”

Our Mission
